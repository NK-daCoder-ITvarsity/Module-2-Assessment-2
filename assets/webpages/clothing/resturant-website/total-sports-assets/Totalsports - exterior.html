Totalsports

Flagship retail store design showcasing the energy and passion of sport and fitness.

1500sqm / Pretoria, South Africa / 2019

Project info

Location:

Menlyn Shopping Mall, Pretoria South Africa

About the client:

TFG is one of the foremost independent chain-store groups in South Africa and has a diverse portfolio of 29 leading fashion retail brands.

What we did:

Interior Design Concept, Technical Documentation, Coordination, Project Management, Compliance.

Award

The ambition for the new Totalsports store design was to create an environment that showcase the energy and passion of sport and fitness, a place for everyone to unlock their potential. Located in one of South Africas largest shopping centres, the store footprint covers over 1500 sqm, making the store the biggest Totalsports in Africa.

The interior design treatment was inspired by the places we practice sports - health clubs, studios and the arena. Ultimately the store interior design is an evolution of the Totalsports brand environment. It aims to be both engaging and inspiring - showing up in a contemporary and energetic way.
